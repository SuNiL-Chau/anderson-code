Let us be clear, at Anderson College we fundamentally understand that your success is our success. You’re the reason we do what we do, and there is no greater joy for us than to see our students thrive in their chosen career and transform their lives to match their dreams.

Our commitment to you is to give you the leading-edge training, tools, inspiration, and support to achieve your goals. And here’s how …

EDUCATION TO EMPOWER

The foundation of our commitment to you is to provide superior education and training, delivered by industry experts, blending theoretical and practical, hands-on learning – giving you every opportunity to become job-ready and in-demand.

Our programs are held to the highest standards, incorporating stakeholder feedback, and obtaining certifications where they serve our students best. Whether new to Canada, or living here for years, seeking a first, second or ideal career our fast-track, high-quality programs and community of support are designed to get you into the workforce quickly and successfully.

When you succeed, we succeed.

STUDENT SERVICES TO SUPPORT

From start to finish, you have access to a team of dedicated, caring professionals committed to guiding you, step-by-step to success.

Admissions

Our team is ready to take inquiry calls, guide you in determining the best program fit for you career goals, and ensure you have all the details you need to launch your dreams for the future with us.

Finance

This expert team connects you to all the information and forms required to navigate your best financial options, empowering you to focus on your education, worry free.

Career Services

Support for you as a student – from resume writing, job search and interview preparation to job fairs and networking opportunities – is available to you, for life!

Mental Wellness Support

We know that for you to achieve success, there may be times when you need support as you navigate school and life. Our keep.MeSAFE service is a virtual, easy-to-use mental health service, offering multilingual support for students 24/7 to help deal with school, health, and other concerns.

EASY ACCESS TO YOUR OUTCOMES

Resumes and Cover Letters

Your resume with a cover letter is your first impression with potential employers. And, just as you are continually evolving, becoming a better and better version of yourself with greater skills and experience, so too is your resume. Right out of the gate, we teach you how to craft a resume you can be proud of and that will make a lasting and compelling impression.

We provide you with workshops, one-on-one counseling, and informative packages that help guide you throughout the creative process and the first steps to finding your next career. With new advanced technologies in resume scanning, we’ll give you the insights and keywords to get your resume noticed.

Job Search

With a resume and cover letter designed to make an impression, now your job is to ensure potential employers have a chance to read it. We’ll guide you through the next part in the process, from selecting job-offerings that align with your goals, to sending out your resume and how to follow up with the HR department in a way that leaves them clear that you are a candidate they should meet.

And know, that just because a job is not posted, doesn’t mean a company is not looking for someone dynamic; just like you. We have more great secrets to success to share with you!

Interview Techniques

You landed the interview! Now what? We’ll walk you through the specifics of how to present yourself with clarity and confidence, prepared to highlight the quality of what you offer and prove you are the best candidate for the job.

From what to wear, how to enter a room and make an impression, to how to shake hands and shine in your answers to all their questions, we prepare you for success.

Job Fairs at Anderson

Every year Anderson College hosts job fairs to connect and empower our students. This is your opportunity to meet and network with potential employers, practice creating a first impression, hone your interview skills and shine.

Your Outcome

Upon completion of the program your credentials, diploma/certificates will be available to you online, accessible, and verifiable from anywhere, anytime!

We have met the needs of our students and the growing demand for secure, instant, access and sharing of your digital credentials. Now your prospective or current employers and clients can quickly view virtually what makes you stand out in a professional crowd.

We’ve also made it possible for you to display your achievements in the best possible light across all platforms. Your certificates and badges are optimized for any screen size and can be shared on social media, safely, through a blockchain. You can edit, manage, verify, share, send and more …
